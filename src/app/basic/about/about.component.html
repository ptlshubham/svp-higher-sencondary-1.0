History

The journey of Charotar English Medium School

With the increasing strength of our students and necessity of space, the school was housed in independent premises named Vitthal Kaka Bhavan in 2005. With the constant support of our stake holders and the management, the school has reflected exponential growth and so a new building came into existence with ultra modern infrastructure in 2011.
